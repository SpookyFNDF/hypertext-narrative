The Dwarves' Goal

It turns out the dwarves are on a grand quest to recover their home in the Lonely Mountain,
for the hoarding dragon Smaug has taken control of their home and their giant pile of treasure within the mountain.
Gandalf has been assisting the dwarves and the mysterious symbols on Bilbo's door were a sign for the dwarves that Bilbo's home should be their meeting place.

Will Bilbo continue his stay in quaint Hobbiton or perhaps seeing the plight of the dwarves has awakened something within him?
